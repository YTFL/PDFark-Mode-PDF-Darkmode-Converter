# PDFark-Mode
**Private & Instant PDF Theme Converter (Dark & Light Mode)**

**PDFark-Mode** is a web application that allows you to instantly convert your PDFs between standard light and elegant dark themes. Perfect for late-night reading or converting dark documents back for traditional viewing.

**Website:** [https://pdfark-mode.vercel.app/](https://pdfark-mode.vercel.app/)

## Why use PDFark-Mode?

- **Bidirectional Conversion**: Seamlessly switch between dark and light themes for any PDF.
- **Privacy First**: Processing happens directly in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server. Your documents are perfectly secure.
- **Modern UI/UX**: Designed for a smooth, app-like experience.
- **Fast and Reliable**: Accurate PDF generation right within your browser.

## How to Use
1. Open the [PDFark-Mode Website](https://pdfark-mode.vercel.app/).
2. Click to upload or drag and drop your PDF file.
3. Wait for the processing to complete.
4. Download your newly generated PDF.
